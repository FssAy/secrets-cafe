Secrets Cafe

Ever wanted to vent off about something you cannot share with anyone around? If so, this is a place for you!
Share your secrets, vent off, wrtie your story, or see what other people posted with full anonymity. You don't have to sign-up, and there are no ads to track you. Each post is verified before getting public to check for misuse, spam, or harmful content, so browse without worries. This is a "pre-release" of this website, bugs and issues are to be expected.
